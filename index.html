Psychoacoustics Robustness : Exploring Audio Processing Robustness

Research Project, 2024



Abstract

This project investigates the robustness of psychoacoustic models against adversarial attacks. We explore how different attack methods can manipulate audio signals while maintaining perceptual similarity, challenging the robustness of human auditory perception and automated audio processing systems. Our research focuses on understanding the vulnerabilities in psychoacoustic models and developing effective countermeasures against these sophisticated audio attacks.
